FAQs

Orders

  1. Can I cancel my order?

    Any cancellations to the order shall be made before 48 hours of the actual time of delivery to claim refund of the paid amount.

    In case of Courier Products cancellation is not possible if the product is dispatched.

    Personalised Products once placed can not be cancelled.

  2. Can I get a refund?

    Refund shall be made under following conditions:

    • he 48hr delivery window has not started.
    • Order was not delivered to the recipient.( This does not include the case for unfounded recipient )
    • If there is any complain regarding the quality of the product being delivered, we will inquire about the same and if it is

      found to be true, the customer will get a full refund or the product will be replaced.

  3. In case of Cancellation, will I get a full refund?

    In case of Cancellation, 5% charge will be applicable.

  4. When will my order be delivered?

    You can choose any one of our delivery schemes:

    • Standard Delivery (Free).
    • Guaranteed 48 hour delivery (Rs. 100).
    • Same day delivery within 3 hours. (Rs. 500).

      • Note: This option is not applicable after 10 PM.
